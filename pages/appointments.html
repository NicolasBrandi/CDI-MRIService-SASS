  • Request an appointment

  • Reques an appointment with Clinic of Diagnostic Imaging at any time on any moment you need. Select request and after completing the requirements we would send you the confirmation of the appointment.

  • While an online request the best way to reach us is contacting us pressing the contact button and following the instractions or calling, you can call any location during operating hours. If this is an emergency, call 911 or your local emergency services.

  • Note: If you are already a patient of Clinic of Diagnostic Imaging, we ask that you also send us this information, so that we can update and verify your data.

  • Reschedule an appointment

  • If you need to reschedule an appointment contact or call any member of the clinic that can help you to change the time of your visit and generate an available time.

  • Please confirm you full name and medical record number. After providing that information one of the members of our staff will get in contact with you to contact the appartment were you need to reschedule and generate a new agenda acording a time that works for you to assist to the clinic.

  • For certain studies, information and preparation for the study is given to you along with the shift. If you were given this documentation, it is important that you read it in advance. In some cases, questionnaires are also given to you, which you must complete
